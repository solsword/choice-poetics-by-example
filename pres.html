Choice Poetics

Choice Poetics

  • A formal framework for understanding narrative choices
  • Concrete language for choice structures
  • A frame that creates edge cases
  • It focuses on discrete & explicit choices
  • It includes a process for analyzing choices mechanically
  • Defines parts of a choice like framing, options, and outcomes
  • If you don’t like formalism, at least it’s interesting to look at the kinds of choices that don’t fit

  • These are the kinds of choices that choice poetics is focused on…

  • Agency

  • etc.

  2. What outcomes are suggested?
  3. What impacts do they have on player goals?
  4. (Afterwards) What were the actual outcomes? Their impacts?

Papers Please

A slide to describe a bit about the game, and how it forces you to make difficult choices, pitting your income + support for your family against the impulse to support desperate but possibly mendacious applicants.

Explain that we’ll use this game to show off a more detailed + mechanical analysis process.

Goal Analysis

  • [high] Avoid the penalty for making mistakes.

So these are the changes if we assume that the applicant is telling the truth about being a refugee: notice how suddenly, we have a hinders in both columns, and that arrangement is recognizable as a component of the dilemma pattern of choice structures.

Of course, an classical dilemma wouldn’t have redeeming features for either option, but we can at least say that the choice has become a difficult one, which we’ll be much less comfortable making.

So what this analysis tells us is that Papers Please is using a very specific choice formula as a rhetorical gesture: it is demonstrating to the player how ethical dilemmas, when seasoned with just a bit of uncertainty, can lead to complicit decisions. -next slide-

Takeaways

  • Dilemma + doubt = complicity
    • Papers Please lets players engage with this directly
    • Repeated uneasy choices build the dystopian atmosphere
  • Detailed choice analysis can yield useful insights
    • Labeling goals × options reveals choice structure
    • Could repeat with different imagined player goals

We don’t have time to go through the outcomes analysis for this choice, but the player never really learns in Papers Please whether the person that they let through was telling the truth or not, which is how the game maintains ambiguity about these decisions.

The main point of this analysis is to show how uncertainty changes the decision landscape for our hypothetical player, and how it turns a difficult decision into an easier decision, where the best option is aligned with the interests of the (fictional) state, because the outcomes for your family are always more immediate and certain than the outcomes for the petitioners you are approving or denying.
